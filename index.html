The 2025 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross

About The Model

The 2025 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross delivers a strong mix of turbocharged performance and everyday practicality. Powered by a 1.5L MIVEC Turbo 4-cylinder with 152 hp and 184 lb-ft of torque, it pairs seamlessly with an 8-speed sport mode CVT for smooth acceleration. Drivers can choose between Front-Wheel Drive or Mitsubishi Super All-Wheel Control for added traction in any condition. Fuel efficiency stays reasonable at around 9.6 L/100 km city and 8.9 L/100 km highway, while a 1,500 lb towing capacity adds versatility when needed.
